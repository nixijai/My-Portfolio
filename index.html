About Me

I am a Industrial Design Student currently studying at Victoria University of Wellington. I am originally from Taranaki, New Zealand. A lot of my design does have an element of my home in it as it is where I found out I wanted to be a designer and where I get most of my inspiration from. Not only am I working on projects for my university assignments, but I am also working on projects for my friends and hopefully potential new clients.

Projets

Here are some of the projects I have been working on for the last year and a half. It is just a brief introduction to my work, but it shows some of my favourtie work I have done so far along with showing some of the skills I have picked up along the way.

The Pen

A pen to change the world. This pen was designed for the signing of the 1893 Electoral Act allowing women to vote in New Zealand.

Mountain and Sea

Bringing both elements together, this project was a model made to represent my home Taranaki. The model shows the beautiful Moint Taranaki and the ocean that surrounds it.

The Hip

An object that rotates and locks. For this project I had to recreate the movement of a hip in my final model.

The Instrument

3D printing is the future. For this project I had to use the 3D printers to create a new type of instrument that would make a new sound..

The Rak

Making storage that much easier. For this project I had to design an object that would help give the room more space. The Rak is a new hanger that sits on the door that can carry up to three objects, keeping things off the floor and nice and tidy.

The Mask

You look like you are from Taranaki. The Mask was a project I did where I 3D printed my face to have Mount Taranaki as my nose.
